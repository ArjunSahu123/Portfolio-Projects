# 🛒 Blinkit Grocery Sales Data Analysis

## Overview

This project presents a business-oriented analysis of Blinkit’s grocery sales data. The objective was to move beyond basic data exploration and build a structured workflow that mirrors real-world analytics — starting from raw data processing to delivering actionable insights through a Power BI dashboard.

The analysis focuses on understanding customer behavior, product performance, and outlet efficiency, with the aim of supporting data-driven business decisions.

---

## Objective

The goal of this project was to analyze Blinkit’s sales data and identify patterns that can help improve business performance.

Key areas of focus include:

* Understanding purchasing behavior across product categories
* Identifying high and low performing outlets
* Evaluating the impact of product attributes like fat content
* Highlighting opportunities to optimize revenue and operations

---

## Tools and Technologies

* **SQL** → Data extraction, filtering, and aggregation
* **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)** → Data cleaning and exploratory analysis
* **Excel** → Initial validation and quick metric checks
* **Power BI** → Dashboard development and visualization

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional and outlet-level information including:

* Product categories and item details
* Sales and revenue data
* Customer ratings
* Outlet characteristics such as size, location, and establishment year

Format: Excel (.xlsx)

---

## Analysis Approach

The workflow followed a structured pipeline:

1. **Data Preparation**

   * Cleaned and validated data using Python
   * Handled missing values and inconsistencies
   * Created derived metrics for analysis

2. **Exploratory Analysis**

   * Identified trends in sales and ratings
   * Compared performance across categories and outlets
   * Validated metrics using Excel

3. **Business Querying (SQL)**

   * Aggregated sales across dimensions
   * Derived KPIs such as total sales and average rating
   * Structured data for dashboard consumption

4. **Visualization (Power BI)**

   * Built an interactive dashboard
   * Designed visuals aligned with business questions
   * Enabled filtering and drill-down for deeper insights

---

## Key Business Questions

The analysis was driven by specific business questions:

* How does fat content influence total sales?
* Which item categories contribute the most to revenue?
* How do different outlet types perform across KPIs?
* Does outlet age impact sales performance?
* Which outlet sizes and locations generate the highest revenue?

---

## Dashboard Overview

The Power BI dashboard provides a consolidated view of all key insights.

It includes:

* KPI cards for quick performance tracking
* Category and outlet-level breakdowns
* Time-based trends using line charts
* Geographic and distribution insights
* Interactive slicers for dynamic analysis

The dashboard is designed to allow stakeholders to quickly identify patterns and drill deeper into specific segments.

---

## Key Insights

* Certain product categories consistently contribute higher revenue, indicating strong demand patterns
* Sales distribution varies significantly across outlet types and sizes
* Customer ratings provide useful signals for product and service quality
* Outlet characteristics such as location and establishment year influence performance
* Some segments show dependency on specific attributes, highlighting optimization opportunities

---

## Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories
* Re-evaluate underperforming outlets and optimize operations
* Use customer ratings to improve product assortment and service quality
* Optimize inventory based on demand patterns
* Align expansion strategies with high-performing outlet characteristics
