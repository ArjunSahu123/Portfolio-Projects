# 🏡 Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard

## 📌 About the Project

This project focuses on analyzing Airbnb data across multiple cities to understand how the platform performs in terms of pricing, listings, user engagement, and growth trends over time.

The goal was not just to build a dashboard, but to tell a story — how Airbnb evolved, what drives pricing, and what patterns exist in user behavior.

---

## 🎯 What I Explored

While building this dashboard, I tried to answer:

* How has Airbnb grown over the years?
* Which cities dominate listings and pricing?
* What type of rooms are most popular?
* How do user reviews behave — frequent vs one-time users?
* What factors influence trust and listing performance?

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Visualization & Storytelling

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

### 🔹 Key Metrics

* **113K+ Listings**
* **10 Cities**
* **78K+ Hosts**
* **136 Property Types**
* **5.3M+ Reviews**

---

### 📈 Growth Over Time

The platform shows a strong **growth phase (2010–2014)**, followed by **maturity and saturation (2015–2016)**.

A noticeable dip appears around **2020**, clearly reflecting the impact of **COVID-19**, after which the market struggles to recover at the same pace.

---

### 🏙️ City-Level Insights

* Cities like **Cape Town and Bangkok** show **higher pricing**, indicating premium demand
* Cities like **Paris and Istanbul** have **high listing counts**, suggesting competitive supply
* Market dynamics differ significantly across cities

---

### 💰 Pricing Analysis

* Pricing varies widely depending on location and demand
* Entire homes and hotel rooms are positioned at the **higher end**
* Shared and private rooms cater to **budget segments**

---

### 🛏️ Room Type Trends

* **Entire homes dominate** the market
* Private rooms follow steady growth
* Shared rooms remain relatively low in demand

---

### 👥 User Engagement Insights

* The review distribution is **highly skewed**
* Majority of users leave only **one review**
* A very small group of users contributes multiple reviews

👉 This suggests:

* Low repeat engagement
* Platform is more **transactional than loyalty-driven**

---

### 🛡️ Trust Analysis

* Listings with **profile pictures and verification** show higher trust levels
* A large portion of listings still lack complete trust indicators

---

### 📊 Listings Distribution

* A few cities dominate total listings
* Long-tail distribution indicates unequal market spread

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Airbnb experienced rapid early growth but is now in a **mature phase**
* Pricing is heavily influenced by **city demand and supply balance**
* User behavior indicates **low retention but high acquisition**
* Trust factors play a role in listing performance
* External events (like COVID-19) significantly impact platform dynamics

---

## 💡 Business Implications

* Focus on improving **user retention and repeat engagement**
* Optimize pricing strategies based on **city-level demand**
* Encourage hosts to complete profiles for better trust
* Identify underperforming cities for expansion opportunities
