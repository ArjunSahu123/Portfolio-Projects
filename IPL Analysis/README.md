# 🏏 Indian Premier League (IPL) Data Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 About the Project

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze the Indian Premier League (IPL) from multiple perspectives — teams, players, and match-level insights.

The aim was to go beyond basic stats and understand patterns in performance, player contributions, and how the league has evolved over time.

---

## 🎯 What I Wanted to Explore

While working on this project, I focused on answering:

* How has IPL evolved over the years?
* Which teams and players have dominated the league?
* What are the trends in scoring and match dynamics?
* How do individual player matchups (batsman vs bowler) perform?
* What factors influence team success and rankings?

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Modeling
* Interactive Visualizations

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

The dashboard is divided into **three main sections**:

---

### 🔹 1. IPL Overview

Provides a high-level understanding of the league:

* Total Editions: **18**
* Total Teams: **16**
* Total Matches: **1100+**

#### Key Insights:

* IPL has shown steady growth in popularity and scale
* Average runs per innings have increased over time, indicating a more aggressive playing style
* Certain stadiums host significantly more matches, acting as key venues

---

### 🔹 2. Team Analysis

Focuses on team performance across seasons:

* Matches played, won, and lost
* Winning percentage by venue
* Seasonal rankings (Top 4 teams each year)

#### Key Insights:

* Some teams show consistent top performance across seasons
* Home venues can significantly impact win percentages
* Rankings fluctuate, showing competitive balance in the league

---

### 🔹 3. Player Analysis

A detailed look into player performance:

#### 🔸 Batsman vs Bowler Faceoff

* Runs scored
* Balls faced
* Strike rate
* Wickets taken

👉 Helps understand **individual matchups**

---

#### 🔸 Top Performers

* Top 5 Batsmen (by runs)
* Top 5 Bowlers (by wickets)
* Top 5 Hitters (by boundaries)

---

#### 🔸 Performance Trends

* Distribution of performance across overs
* Seasonal comparison of player impact

---

#### 🔸 Trust & Player Profiling

* Analysis based on player consistency and contributions
* Helps identify reliable performers

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* IPL has become increasingly **batting-friendly over time**
* A few players consistently dominate across seasons
* Match outcomes can heavily depend on **key player matchups**
* Teams with balanced batting and bowling units perform better
* Middle overs play a crucial role in controlling the game

---

## 💡 What This Means

From a practical perspective:

* Teams should focus on **data-driven player matchups**
* Venue and pitch conditions should influence strategy
* Identifying consistent performers is key for team stability
* Data can be used for **better team selection and match planning**
