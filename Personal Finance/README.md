# 💰 Personal Finance Dashboard

## 📌 About the Project

This project is a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze personal financial data, focusing on income, expenses, savings, and overall net worth over time.

The goal was to understand spending behavior, identify saving patterns, and evaluate whether financial decisions align with income trends.

---

## 🎯 What I Wanted to Explore

While building this dashboard, I focused on answering:

* How is my income distributed over time?
* What percentage of income is spent vs saved?
* Where does most of the money go (expenses)?
* How effectively am I saving and investing?
* Is my spending aligned with my income growth?

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

* **Total Income:** ₹1.51M
* **Expense Ratio:** 78%

---

### 📈 Income vs Expense Trends

* Tracks how income, expenses, and savings change over time
* Helps evaluate whether spending is proportional to earnings

---

### 💸 Expense Distribution

Breakdown of where money is spent:

* House Rent (~40%)
* Groceries & Food (~24%)
* EMIs (~21%)
* Health, Leisure, Shopping (smaller portions)

👉 Shows that fixed expenses dominate overall spending

---

### 💰 Savings Distribution

Analysis of how savings are allocated:

* Mutual Funds (~71%)
* Emergency Fund (~14%)
* Fixed Deposits (~13%)
* Liquid Cash (~minimal)

👉 Indicates a strong focus on investment-based savings

---

### 📊 Detailed Financial Statement

* Year-wise breakdown of income, expenses, and savings
* Helps track financial growth and consistency over time

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* A large portion of income is spent on **fixed expenses (rent + EMIs)**
* Savings are heavily focused on **mutual funds**, indicating long-term investment strategy
* Expense ratio (~78%) suggests scope for better cost control
* Income and spending trends show fluctuations that impact savings consistency

---

## 💡 What This Means

From a practical perspective:

* Reducing fixed expenses can significantly improve savings rate
* Diversifying savings beyond mutual funds can reduce risk
* Tracking spending patterns helps in better financial planning
* Aligning expenses with income growth is crucial for wealth building
