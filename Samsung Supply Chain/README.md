# 📦 Supply Chain & Logistics Dashboard (Samsung)

## 📌 About the Project

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the supply chain and logistics performance of a consumer electronics company (Samsung).

The objective was to understand how different components of the supply chain — suppliers, inventory, shipments, and customers — contribute to overall business performance, and where inefficiencies or opportunities exist.

---

## 🎯 What I Wanted to Explore

While building this dashboard, I focused on key business questions:

* How efficient is the supply chain in terms of delivery and lead time?
* Which suppliers are performing well and which are causing delays?
* How is inventory distributed across products?
* Which customers generate the most revenue?
* What factors impact profit and order fulfillment?

---

### 🚚 Supply Chain Components

The dashboard is structured into four main areas:

* **Supplier** → Performance & lead times
* **Inventory** → Product-level stock value
* **Shipment** → Delivery and delays
* **Customer** → Revenue contribution

---

## 📈 Key Analysis

### ⏱️ Lead Time by Supplier

* Highlights how long each supplier takes to deliver
* Helps identify slow suppliers affecting operations

---

### 📦 Inventory Value by Product

* Shows which products hold the highest inventory value
* Useful for managing stock and avoiding over/under inventory

---

### 🚛 Carrier Performance (Delay Analysis)

* Compares logistics carriers based on total delay
* Identifies bottlenecks in delivery

---

### 💰 Revenue by Customer

* Displays top customers contributing to revenue
* Helps prioritize high-value clients

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* A few suppliers contribute significantly to higher lead times
* Inventory is concentrated in certain high-value products
* Some logistics carriers consistently cause delays
* Revenue is driven by a small group of major customers
* Perfect order rate (~75%) indicates room for operational improvement

---

## 💡 Business Implications

From a practical standpoint:

* Optimize or replace suppliers with high lead times
* Improve inventory planning to reduce holding costs
* Choose logistics partners based on performance data
* Strengthen relationships with high-revenue customers
* Focus on improving order fulfillment processes
